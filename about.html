About Zucchini

Zucchini was born out of a simple observation: most of us have perfectly good ingredients sitting in our kitchens that eventually go to waste because we don't know what to make with them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower home cooks to be more creative, sustainable, and economical. Food waste is a global challenge, and we believe that technology can play a small but significant role in solving it. By helping you find the perfect recipe for those three carrots and a half-carton of heavy cream, we're helping you save money and the planet.

How It Works

Zucchini uses a sophisticated (yet simple to use) algorithm that matches your available ingredients against a curated database of thousands of high-quality recipes. We prioritize recipes that use the maximum number of your input ingredients while requiring the fewest additional items.

Meet the Team

We are a small team of food enthusiasts and software engineers dedicated to making the kitchen a less stressful and more enjoyable place. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner, Zucchini is designed to be your go-to sous-chef.
